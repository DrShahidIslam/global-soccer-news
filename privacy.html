Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Global Soccer Alerts. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website and associated applications.

2. Pinterest API Usage & Compliance

Our application integrates with the Pinterest API to automate the sharing of soccer-related content and alerts. Please note the following important details regarding our use of Pinterest's services:

  • Pinterest API Data: We explicitly use the Pinterest API to publish content to Pinterest boards on behalf of authorized accounts.
  • Non-Affiliation: Global Soccer Alerts is an independent product and is not endorsed by or affiliated with Pinterest in any way.
  • Data Handling & Disconnection: If a user disconnects their Pinterest account from our service or revokes our application's access, all Pinterest-derived data associated with that user is immediately and permanently deleted from our systems.
  • No Resale or Redistribution: We strictly confirm that we do not resell or redistribute Pinterest content, or any Pinterest-derived data, to third parties.

3. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal data from visitors to this bridge page unless explicitly provided through contact forms. For connected accounts, we only process the necessary OAuth tokens required to publish content to authorized platforms.

4. Use of Information

Any information collected is used solely to provide the core functionality of our service (automating content alerts).
